# ISO 27001 – Access Management

## Overview
Access management ensures only authorized users can access critical systems. Weak access controls caused the [Capital One 2019 breach](https://www.cnet.com/tech/tech-industry/capital-one-breach-everything-you-need-to-know/).

## Controls
- ISO 27001: A.9.1 & A.9.2  
- NIST CSF: PR.AC-1  
- GDPR: Art. 32  
- POPIA: Sec. 19

## Implementation
Terraform IAM roles, least privilege, MFA, and access logging.
